Why consolidate

Replace fragmented gateway stacks with one governed traffic layer.

Separate tools for APIs, AI, events, MCP, integrations, and portals create policy drift, blind spots, and slow delivery. WASL gives teams one place to define control, one place to observe traffic, and one place to publish secure digital products.

Fragmented stack
  • Different policies for every traffic type
  • Separate portals, logs, and ownership models
  • AI and MCP tool access outside normal controls
  • Harder audits, slower releases, more operational cost
WASL platform
  • One governance layer for APIs, AI, events, MCP, and integrations
  • Unified Studio, portal, traces, policy, and analytics
  • Built-in security, privacy, guardrails, and approvals
  • Hybrid-ready delivery without duplicating platforms
